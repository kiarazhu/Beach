Beaches in Australia have signs to help you understand whether it is safe to swim or not:

Red & Yellow Flags: Swim between the flags. This area will be safe to swim in. Red Flag: No Swimming.
Yellow Flag: Caution required. Potential hazards. Red & White Flag: Evacuate the water.
Black & White Flag: Surfcraft riding area boundary.
Warning Swimming not advised Large waves Marine Stingers
